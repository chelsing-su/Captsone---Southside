Projects Overview

The Southside Community Coalition is working on several projects in the Syracuse Community. These projects serve the purpose of restoring, revitalizing, and rejuvenating the Syracuse's south side community. They all have sustainable and long term benefits for the community. These projects are being worked on by community residents who have taken initiative to make sure that their community has a brighter future. Please take a look at all the projects that we are currently involved in. We are always looking for help so feel free to get involved and contact us.

Take a look at some of the great work we've been able to accomplish alongside other great organizations in our community!
